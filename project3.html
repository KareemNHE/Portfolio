Sport Social Media Android Mobile Application

About

Overview: The Shot is a sports-focused social media app built for fans, athletes, coaches, beginners, or people who simply want to learn a party trick safely! It’s designed to bring people together through their shared passion for sport — by posting videos, sharing highlights, and connecting in a supportive environment. The goal? To inspire, inform, and empower.

My Role

I developed the full app using Flutter - within the MVVM architecture - and Firebase - for the backend - within Android Studios. It works just like modern platforms users are used to — with posts, videos, likes, hashtags, and follows. But everything is filtered by sport, tailored to user interest, and supported by real-time news and private messaging.

Key Features & Technologies

  • Android Studios
  • Flutter
  • Kotlin
  • MVVM
  • Dart
  • Firebase
